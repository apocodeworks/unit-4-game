Crystal Collector




You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals above. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the given number. You lose if your score surpasses that number.
The value of each crystal is randomized each round.
Good luck!
